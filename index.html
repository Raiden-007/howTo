Cereal

Ingredients

  • 1 glass bowl
  • cereal of your choice
  • milk
  • spoon

Steps

  1. Get a glass bowl
  2. Pour your cereal into the bowl
  3. Pour milk into the bowl
  4. Grab a spoon
  5. Put your spoon into the cereal bowl
  6. Enjoy your cereal while watching some fire cartoons
  7. When done, put the bowl in the sink
  8. Wash the bowl
  9. Then you are all done!

PB&J Sandwhich

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly
  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 butter knife
  • 1 plate
  • Chips

Steps

  1. Get a plate
  2. Put 2 slices of bread on the plate
  3. Grab the butter knife
  4. Open the peanut butter jar
  5. Spread peanut butter on one slice of bread
  6. Close the peanut butter jar
  7. Open the jelly jar
  8. Spread jelly on the other slice of bread
  9. Close the jelly jar
  10. Put the two slices together
  11. Grab a bag of chips and enjoy!
  12. When done, put the plate in the sink

Pepperoni Pizza

  • Naan bread
  • Pizza sauce
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Olive oil
  • 1 pizza pan
  • 1 pizza cutter
  • 1 oven

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees
  2. Get a pizza pan
  3. Put the naan bread on the pizza pan
  4. Soread olive oil on the bread
  5. Spread pizza sauce on the naan bread
  6. Put cheese on top of the pizza sauce
  7. Add pepperoni on top of the cheese
  8. Put the pizza in the oven for 10 minutes
  9. When done, take it out and let it cool for 5 minutes
  10. Cut it into slices with a pizza cutter
  11. Enjoy!
